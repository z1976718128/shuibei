About ICA

ICA introduction

International Colored Gemstone Association (ICA) was founded in 1984. It is a non-profit organization and the only worldwide body which specifically serves the global Colored Gemstone industry and devotes to promoting the knowledge of colored gemstones and connoisseurship. Since the founding of ICA, it has built a global network of its membership that works together to develop a common language for promotion and consistent ethical business standards necessary to improve international communication and trade of colored gemstones. ‘World's Colors Glitter in China’ would be the topic of ICA Congress in September 2020 and the journey will start from Shenzhen, China.
